18 Ranks

An ordinary Earth Wysp gains 18 ranks (3 HD: 6 Outsider, 0 int). These ranks are immovably put in Bluff, Climb, Knowledge (dungeoneering, engineering, planes, 2 ranks each), Perception, Sense Motive.

Familiars use their master's skill ranks if they are better, but always uses their modifiers. This familiar own's ranks will be bold.

Familiars do not gain HD (they just count as having more HD), so they don't gain skill ranks with HD. This also affects the maximum number of ranks that can be put in a skill. However, they do gain ranks when their int increases.

Familiars always treat Acrobatics, Climb, Fly, Perception, Stealth, Swim as class skills. Outsiders always treat Bluff, Craft, Knowledge (Planes), Perception, Sense Motive, Stealth as class skills. Earth Wysps treat Knowledge (dungeoneering, engineering) as class skills.
