Ulysses found a magic spirit in a weird jar buried in his grandfather's backyard, when he was a teen. He named the spirit "Mini", and it doesn't really speak, but it's been following him around since, in a somewhat cat-like or fairy-like fashion. Mini often hides in Ulysses's weapon, amplifying the magic as needed and unleashing powerful and unexpected potential.

Ulysses has been rising very fast through the ranks of the guild of the Lance Étincellante 👑, and is a very promising adventurer, though he lacks some experience.

He's currently in St-Trimothée on a quest to fight some ghoul-like drakes that harvest automatic convoys. He's heard that someone in town might have a spirit similar to Mini, and would like to learn more.
