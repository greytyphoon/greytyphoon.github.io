Rii's connection to the plane of Air is hereditary. It was discovered early in childhood and he received tutoring to properly master it. Rii's undeniably gifted at it! The tribe is most proud.

He inherited an odd quirk that most of his mentors don't realise, though: during storm nights where his powers are fullest, Rii feels the rush to steal, to satisfy his desires, to prove his powers make him uncatchable. Nobody's ever caught him. It's pretty harm to blame him when he can turn to literal lightning to rush out of sight.

Rii's recently stolen something bigger than usual. He feels guilty about it, but can't put it back. Maybe it's just his conscience, but he feels watched since then. Taking an adventuring contract, Rii figures he'll be away for a while to draw attention off, while doing something good to redeem himself.
