A physician by trade, a prestigious position in his homeland: medicine was seen as the right of the monarchy, and all physicians were approved by the king (sometimes indirectly). Hubert was specialised in physical therapy, rather than ingredients and concoctions, and drew inspiration from martial arts in his treatment. In the university-hospital where he did most of his work, a new controversial nomination was announced, and Hubert resigned as protest. Now he is looking for a good place to found his own school-hospital to pass on his knowledge. And let's be honest, he's in great shape of body and mind, might as well make some money while he's looking!
