Notes de campagne

Playing the campaign

  • The goal is to rush through the books, focusing mainly on fights.
  • Mode sans merci. Le but du GM est de tuer, mais pas de TPK, id√©alement tuant n-1 joueurs.
  • "Quicksave" after each fight
  • As soon as we have downtime & we are level 4+, start every fight fully healed
  • No time limit on turns, mais on fait comme si
  • Random traps will simply be skipped
  • Puzzles can be skipped or not, at the GM's discretion. After a while the GM should just give obvious hints to keep going.
  • Random encounters will generally be skipped, at the GM's discretion. Usually we keep if harder than average, story-important, or otherwise unique.
  • We plan games far in advance, so there should rarely be any absent.
  • If one player is absent, we play anyway. We might bring in a replacement.
  • If two players are absent, cancel.

Hero Points

Our hero points can
  1. auto-stabilize at 0 HP monsters think you died,
  2. reroll any d20, keep the new result.
Player reset at one hero points:
  1. When a session starts,
  2. After every third encounter.

Character Creation

  • 25 point-buy
  • HD are always average
  • XP milestone
  • 2 traits, no drawbacks
  • Only 1PP Paizo, no 3PP, no homebrew

Wealth

  • We don't gain items from loot, searching, etc.
  • We gain wealth by level.
  • We gain it naturally, immediately when we level up.
  • At level one, all characters have 175 gold.
  • If your character has a spellbook and you want to learn more spells than those you would normally have by levelling up, you can.
    Increase the cost of your spellbook by the cost of learning a spell of that level.
  • We can have up to 10% of total wealth as consumables.
    These do not replenish between fights or during downtime.
    On level up, the slate is cleaned. New consumables can be purchased up to the new wealth by level.

Houserules

  1. EWP is a trait (simplifies Heirloom Weapon shenanigans)

Item creation/crafting in downtime is not supported.

  • Wizards get spell focus instead of scribe scroll.
  • Alchemists get extra bombs instead of brew potion.
  • Skalds get extra performance instead of scribe scroll.
  • If your class or archetype would give you an item creation feat, refer to Pathfinder Society Rules to see what you get instead.

Banned

  • Leadership, too strong, too slow in play
  • Sacred Geometry, too strong, too slow in play
  • Drawbacks, too easy to ignore
  • Races with >15RP (see with GM)
  • Monsters as PC
