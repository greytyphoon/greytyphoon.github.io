Forgotten Trick is way too strong. Here are some ideas in how to use it.
  • Gain Combat Trick, to gain Wave Strike and you can feint as a swift action at the start of a fight (useful if you rolled awfully on init).
  • Gain Sudden Disguise, on the one time where you would actually use it. (For 1 ki, cast disguise self as a swift action, lasting @lvl minutes)
  • Gain Acrobatic Master, on the one time when you really need +20 Acrobatics for 1 ki.
  • Gain feather fall, 1 ki, immediate action
  • Gain Shadow Clone, and summon those 1d4 mirror images! 1 ki, standard action.
  • Gain Darkvision, just in time! 60ft, 1 ki, 1 hour
  • Gain Slow Metabolism, just before diving or when you get poisoned
