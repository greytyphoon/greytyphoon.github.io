Share spells (Ex)
The druid may cast a spell with a target of "You" on her animal companion (as a touch range spell) instead of on herself. A druid may cast spells on her animal companion even if the spells normally do not affect creatures of the companion's type (animal). This ability does not allow the animal to share abilities that are not spells, even if they function like spells.
