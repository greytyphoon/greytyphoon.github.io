Mounted Combat Rules
Ammunition

You can remove the Mounted part to turn him easily into a Hooded Champion instead!
human > Mounted Combat > Point-blank Shot
level 1 > Mounted Archery > Precise Shot
ranger 2 > Precise Shot > Rapid Shot
level 9 > Trick Riding > Extra Panache
level 13 > Ranged Trip > Clustered Shot
This build counts on the "Minimum 1" Panache Pool size.
Up to level 9, you don't get real bonuses from keeping 1 in your panache pool (+2 init and getting up without AoO, cool but skippable).
At level 9, the bonuses get better, so we pick up Extra Panache.
We also add Clustered Shot, now that we meet the prerequisites.

The only reason we had a shortbow is because you can't use a longbow while mounted, so switch back to a longbow. And maybe get a wolf?
