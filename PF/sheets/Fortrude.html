Fortrude was born in the Ruby family, a family of noble blood, living in Caxton. She had an identical twin sister, but she died in labor. Her soul, however, kept her connection to Fortrude, and even as a baby she knew how to summon the one who came to be known as Fortune.

Her family suffered the reversals of politics when tackling the corruption in Caxton, and they were slaughtered by the lackeys of Salomon. Those lackeys did not know of the powers of baby Fortrude, and they killed Fortune instead, leaving the real baby alive.

Dostaek d'Onyx was the one who found her after the massacre, and realizing the danger she was still in, quickly took her away, renaming her to PeridotBrander (a rather common name).

When Fortrude learned of this, her rage knew no bound. She grew apart from her childhood friends trying to figure out a way to right the wrongs in Caxton, decided to return one day. Sensing her determination, her fury, her need, and her noble blood, an envoy of Asmodeus proposed a plan to infiltrate the town with an army of lawful devils, ready to right the wrongs of the past. With growing powers as a summoner, and her secret double as a hidden asset, she returned.

Now that her plan is in motion, she is slightly more friendly and amenable. She's become friends with Rolf and Malo of the Jealous Unicorn as well as Vincent Flintforger, often trading publicity. She visits Dostaek often, but claims they're not that close, if only to protect him in case things go wrong.

The next part of her plan involves testing her gladiators to the point where they must reveal if they are undead, and if they aren't, she'll recruit them.
