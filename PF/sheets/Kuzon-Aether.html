Guide NJolly, Guide 3rd

Kuzon (pronounced "Koo-zan", formerly Kjan) is a very young human, who spent a century dormant after being abducted by a fey.

He was born in St-Élie, and his parents noticed his magical abilities. He was placed under the tutelage of a local sorcerer, a kind soul with a forest-based bloodline. Because of this man and his good reputation, his talent was not seen as a liability or a danger.

He doesn't have much life experience (outside limited herbalism knowledge). Alone in a new world, with no family left, he clings to the guild as his new anchor. He is really insecure, often scared, and remains very weak for his age.
