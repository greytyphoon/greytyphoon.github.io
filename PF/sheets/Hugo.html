Hugo is a long-time resident of St-Élie and is a teacher in town. He's really good with a bow, and very patient with kids.

He has deep respect for Oletha, and a friendly church rivalry with Gabriel & Louise Levasseur. He acts as the head priest of Erastil in town.

His relationship with Sargaro, Tarken and Destiny are a little bit less good: he has a harder time trusting people not born in the village.

Ghaveri somehow is the exception to this rule: he has been his prized pupil, basically his adopted son. Hugo presided the ceremony where Ghaveri got his primal powers and his animal companion.
