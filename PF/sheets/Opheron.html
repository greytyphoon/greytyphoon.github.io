Ophéron is a member of the Silki Clan who refused most of his heritage and place in the clan in favor of a druidic apprenticeship, earning the title "The savage one". His mentor, however, trained him in the ways of Gozreh, sensing his bond to the god. He still honors for clan with his fishing skills. When wild shaped, he tends to adopt shapes of fishing animals, like an osprey (balbuzard) or a grizzly bear.
