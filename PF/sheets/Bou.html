The druids of Magaambya have attempted to raise a crop of Leshys as helpers, but he was found wiser than most, and granted status as a full-fledged student. Seemingly, just to see what happens. He's a good sport about it, goes with the flow, yet his magic is always wildly unexpected.

Originally named Doigts d'échardes, but it was a mouthful.
