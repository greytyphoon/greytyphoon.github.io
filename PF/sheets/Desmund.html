Desmund is the brother of Sigmund, and they adventured together for many years. Now they have retired from the adventuring life, and Desmund really hoped for a peaceful time, but his brother's patron is demanding more. Desmund owes his brother his life, multiple times over, and will assist however he can, despite not being directly involved.
