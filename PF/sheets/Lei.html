Our background

Zimo met me when she spared my life in the invasion of my village, and we ran away together. I was badly injured, but we ran into Lana, who could heal me. We moved towards Ba Sing Se, to be safe from the war, but were denied entry.

We became part of a smuggling ring that we named "The Path Finders", sometimes using the codename "Providence". It is masterminded by Willow, and a key piece of it is the blind eye that Zixin turns to us.

Long Feng discovered our smuggling operation and made a proposition that we couldn't refuse. He wants us to bring back Onomu, a fire nation general who stole important war plans from the army. If we succeed, we all get official Ba Sing Se citizenship and our crimes will be overlooked.

Onomu is currently in the village of West Haiatu. She is very famous, and will be easy to recognize.
