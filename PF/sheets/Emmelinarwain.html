Named from Emmelin (Yellowhammer, a type of bird) and Narwain (January) in this Sindarin dictionary.
For the same reason, his animal companions are named Heredir (bird) and Blâbnar (bat).

Emmelinarwain is a soldier of the elven clan.

From Montariz, he gave up his name (Gaël Glenn) to pursue the wilderness instead of his arcane studies.

Got a new name from helping the elves near Larfenil.

Started bounty-hunting when a prize was placed on clear enemies of his goddess, Yueral. Found that it was well-suited to his talents and desire for justice, so he's been doing that when money ran short.
