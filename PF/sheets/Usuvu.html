Rules to play Usuvu

  1. Any player can spend an action to Command Usuvu. She gains two actions. The player chooses how to spend those actions.
  2. At the top of each round, Usuvu becomes ready to act. She can act only once per round.
  3. At the end of each round, if Usuvu hasn't acted, she will Raise Shield and Sustain any spell she had cast.
  4. Usuvu can use a reaction each round. The GM will decide how and when they are spent, but usually, the first trigger to come up will be used.
