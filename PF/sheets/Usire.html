Usire is a defender of the forest. He trained in St-Élie under Hugo. He has two siblings: Vaul and Naomi.

He tamed his bear himself, forging a bond of faith with a wild animal that was threatening other trainees of Erastil's church. It was his god's doing, certainly. Perhaps Erastil blessed him with a strong protector, or entrusted him with a dangerous beast's care? Usire can feel Galléon's animal fury still boiling inside sometimes, but Usire is sure he can keep it contained.

Village life isn't exactly the best when you have a bear companion, so he joined a guild to adventure, do good, and keep Galléon busy.
