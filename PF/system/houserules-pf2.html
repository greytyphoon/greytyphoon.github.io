Pathfinder 2nd-edition Houserules

  • Start at level 4.
    XP will be shared across all players. Everybody will always have exactly the same XP.
  • Start with 1× 4th-level item, 2× 3rd-level items, 2× 2nd-level items, 2× 1st-level items, and 50 gold.
  • Free Archetype
  • One of your class's fixed skills is automatically at maximum proficiency.
  • If you're not moving on your turn, you can get a free Step.
  • [Incapacitation] turns crit failures into normal failures, but nothing more.
  • Exploration actions will cause tension to be added to the pool.

Allowed Material

  • Official Paizo content is always allowed.
  • Third-party content and homebrews will generally be allowed, but check with the GM first.
