PF2 One-Shot: The McAllister Mansion

This is made for a party of level 5, with these houserules.

Convocation

Vous êtes des habitants du village de Fondcomble, et vous avez un peu la gueule de bois. Hier, c'était la Veille de Noël, et vous avez fêté avec vos amis du village et avec vos familles. En vous réveillant ce matin, vous avez découvert le drame: aucun enfant du village n'a eu de cadeau cette année. Par contre, la rumeur se répand rapidement: les enfants du manoir McAllister, eux, en ont reçu... En tant qu'aventuriers qui aviez pris vos retraites pour vous consacrer à vos familles, vous décidez de prendre le choses en main. Vous vous retrouvez à la tombée de la nuit devant la grille du manoir McAllister pour en avoir le coeur net...

Each character should prepare the name of a kid in their responsibility, and what that kid wanted for Christmas.

  4. Hazard 4 — Steam Vents

  6. Hazard 6 — False Step Floor

Plan

  1. Infiltrate the mansion, avoiding Kleine's traps
  2. Discourage rest between traps by having 2× Scarecrows patrolling
  3. Fight to free the elves

NPCs

Kleine McAllister
A bratty, spoiled kid. Forgotten home by his parents for Christmas, he decided to take revenge by stealing everyone's gifts. He's a smart trapper, too, so he was confident in his ability to trap the elves, so long as he could be shielded from view by his older sister's magic.
Anabel McAllister
A quiet, know-it-all, snobby girl. She is Kleine's old sister, who would rather be left alone. Kleine's plan was just a selfish grab for toys, but Anabel saw a great opportunity to tap into the elves' magic, so she agreed to help.
Skipper Gigglynose
Molly Gingershoes
Carol Startoes
Three elves that have been captured by the McAllister kids
