Manifest in 1 minute, dismiss as a safe standard action.

Shared Consciousness (Su) 1/day

As a safe immediate action when you fail vs a mind-affecting effect and your phantom is confined, shunt that effect to your phantom. You can't manifest it and don't gain phantom bonuses for the duration of the effect, but you aren't affected.
