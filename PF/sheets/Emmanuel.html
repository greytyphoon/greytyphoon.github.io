Emmanuelle was given that name at birth by the family of her mother, Colombe Beauregard, who died in labor. Sadly, it is not rare that the mother of a dhampir dies. Few knew that the child was a dhampir, but Gaspar, the priest of Sarenrae of delivered Emmanuelle, knew, and he tried to cleanse the child and save the mother with magic, with no luck.

During her childhood, no one knew for sure if she was really a dhampir. Maybe the cleansing had worked, after all. But puberty eventually revealed her undead potential. Even worse, Sylvestre, her vampiric father, showed up to claim her as his heir, meaning to involve her in his maleficient schemes.

Emmanuelle ran away, with the help of Sarenrae's clergy who covered the tracks. She learned Sarenrae's teachings and earned her favor (plus minor divine powers) during this month-long escape. She also came to terms with her transexuality, and began refering to himself as Emmanuel (he/him/his).

The escape came to a halt near Monktown, when Emmanuel, Evans a clerc of Sarenrae, and Lohim a gnomish gunslinger were attacked by undeads. Emmanuel's natural immunities saved him, but the others were killed. Emmanuel picked up Lohim's weapons to chase the ghouls. He then swore that he would hunt the undead, get stronger, and someday also strike his father down.

He became a vigilante in and around Monktown under the name of Zenaida (in honor of his mother, and of Sarenrae's love for doves). He gained experience and power, and kept his eyes on St-Ãlie to avenge his mother someday. He has recently been recuited by the Fire Drakes guild.
