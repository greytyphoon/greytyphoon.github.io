Patricia is the niece of Gabriel and Louise Levasseur, and she trained under their tutelage towards the faith of Sarenrae. She's quite new in town and doesn't know that many people. She bonded quickly with William over far-away adventuring stories, and joined the Lance Étincellante 👑 guild.

She used to be great friends with Hera but they had a falling-off lately, and Hera joined an opposing guild out of spite. Patricia is deeply sadened by this, in the end, and keeps a trident Hera offered her as a memento.
