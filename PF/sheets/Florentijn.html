Trained classically as a court mage and advisor, Florentijn was pushed off his stable employment by some ambitious whippersnapper. He spent lots of efforts, and most of his not-so-large wealth, proving his innocence and driving off the conman, but now felt he couldn't return to the court that so easily shunned him. He's decided to give freelancing/adventuring a try until he gets back to his feet.
