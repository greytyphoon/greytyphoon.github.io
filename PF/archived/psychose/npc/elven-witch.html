FACTIONS
The Party: Mathilda believes they are sort of foolish, and will be mildly surprised to see them still alive.
Ranger's Guild: Engar has a few contacts in there, and sometimes gets the scoop on the quests that they have to offer. He believes that they are too organised to be truly free, or safe, so he has declined to join. Anyway, he was not quite qualified.
Circle of the Druids: Engar does not really care about them. He usually hears that they are a lot of trouble, so he won't have a problem with accepting a mission that hurts them.
Clergy of Abadar: Rumor has it they are corrupted, and not in a way that advantages Engar. He sees them as judges, and thus he simply tries not to cross their path. If he gets to look nice though, he won't pass. He'd jump on the chance of being on the other side of the corrupted system, too.
Clergy of Pharasma: Engar likes the secrecy of the clergy of Pharasma and, even though he's currently worshipping Shellyn, tries to prove himself to this group.
Dwarves of King Taggrick: They usually pay a pretty penny! Engar has no privileged contact though, and is not looking for one: he believes the kingdom is on its last legs, and might fall in a war anytime.
Duergars of King Murghol: Duergars have a reputation for killing other races without mercy: Engar knows better than to try to get an alliance. However, he wouldn't pass on a quest from them: they're also known for their honor.
The Devils Invasion: Engar has heard of Cheliax, and its devil princes. Devils can technically be trusted, and aren't a problem per se. If they're trying to seize control of the region though, they might be hostile.
The Demon Machinations: Engar really doesn't like demons, and when proof of their presence rears its head, he'll join any coallition against them. Hopefully the pay will be good, too.
